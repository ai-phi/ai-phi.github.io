To close the year, we are gathering for a short and informal ai-phi community session before moving to dinner nearby.

We will take a moment to look back at what the community has built this year: the sessions, conversations, causeries, posts, and connections that have shaped this cycle of ai-phi.

We will also look ahead. What are we preparing for next year? Which formats should we strengthen? Which questions should we make room for? What would people like to see, try, host, discuss, or contribute?

This session is meant as a collective checkpoint: part reflection, part brainstorm, part practical invitation into the next phase of the community.

## On the agenda

- A short review of this year's ai-phi achievements.
- A preview of what is being prepared for next year.
- An open brainstorm on future sessions, causeries, collaborations, and formats.
- A practical explanation of how to contribute to our causerie posts.
- Time to continue the conversation over dinner at a nearby Indian restaurant.

Everyone is welcome, whether you have joined many ai-phi sessions already or are discovering the community now.

## About causerie posts

Causeries are intentionally open and discussion-based. After some causeries, we publish collective posts that gather short reflections, questions, references, or follow-up thoughts from participants.

During this session, we will explain how those contributions work: what kind of text is useful, how informal it can be, how individual voices are preserved, and how the final post becomes a shared trace of the discussion without turning the causerie into a recorded lecture.
